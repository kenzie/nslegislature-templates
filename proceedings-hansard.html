Hansard is an edited record of what was said in the Legislature. It also includes votes, written ministerial statements and written answers to parliamentary questions.

The Hansard Office is responsible for the compliation of a complete and accurate verbatim record of the Debates of the House of Assembly, and of the proceedings of Committees of the House as may be required both when the House is in session and between sessions. The Hansard Editor is Robert Kinsman.
